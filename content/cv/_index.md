## Research Interests
 
- Paleogeographic reconstruction and paleo-river evolution
- Provenance analysis and single-grain analysis
- Geological evolution of the China Seas and Southeast Asia

**Previous Research**  
- Cenozoic topographic evolution and large-river development along the southeastern Tibetan Plateau, including the Pearl River and Red River
- Basin-to-regional source-to-sink system evolution in the South China Sea from rifting to post-rift stages
- Transition from a Mesozoic active margin to a Cenozoic passive margin around the South China Sea region

**Current Research**  
- Paleogeographic reconstruction of the South China Sea and Southeast Asia
- Paleo-Pacific subduction along the South China-Sundaland margin
- New provenance analysis methods
- Transfer and preservation of provenance signals

## Recent Projects

- **Fundamental Research Funds for the Central Universities**, China University of Geosciences (Wuhan): *Mesozoic sediment provenance evolution of the Palawan continental block, South China Sea region*, 2019-05 to 2022-04, completed, PI.
- **Open project, State Key Laboratory of Marine Geology, Tongji University**: *Detrital provenance records of early Paleo-Pacific subduction and paleogeographic reconstruction in the South China Sea region*, 2021-07 to 2023-06, completed, PI.
- **Industry project, CNOOC China Limited Hainan Branch**: *Source-to-sink systems, depositional systems, and large lithologic trap prediction in deep-water middle-deep strata of the Qiongdongnan Basin*, 2021-11 to 2023-07, completed, participant.
- **National Natural Science Foundation of China, Young Scientists Fund**: *Paleo-Pacific subduction records in northeastern Borneo from detrital provenance and chronology of the Baliojong oceanic plate stratigraphy*, 2022-01 to 2024-12, completed, PI.
- **Industry project, CNOOC China Limited Zhanjiang Branch**: *Origin and effectiveness identification of lithologic traps in Weixinan and Wushi depressions*, 2022-11 to 2024-11, completed, participant.
- **Open project, Hebei Engineering Research Center for Marine Coastline Ecological Restoration and Smart Ocean Monitoring**: *Single-grain quartz analysis of the Qinhuangdao coastal zone and implications for sediment transport*, 2025-07 to 2027-07, ongoing, PI.
- **National Natural Science Foundation of China, General Program**: *Late Cretaceous-Paleogene sediment provenance and paleogeographic reconstruction of the eastern Proto-South China Sea margin*, 2026-01 to 2029-12, ongoing, PI.
